About Us

The ideation of this concept came to our minds when we saw many individuals facing this problem of selling their favorite items over the web. Yes, we have many portals which allows you to sell second hand items but imagine a portal with your own dashboard and a web-page which allows you to sell anything that is favorite to you.

Easebuzz is a Symbiosis incubated startup that was started in the year 2016. Both Rohit Prasad & Vikram Kumar (cofounders) are Symbiosis postgraduate students who have years of industrial exposure before they ventured full time on their own startup. They are always passionate to bring in new ideas to help facilitate SMBs.
